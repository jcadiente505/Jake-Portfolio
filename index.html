About Me

Hello my name is Jacob Cadiente! I am a student at the UCF Coding Bootcamp for Full Stack Web Development. I am well versed in Front End and Back End languages and frameworks such as HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript as well as JS frameworks such as Node.js and React.js.


To me, Web Development is the perfect blend of technical skill, logical thinking and creativity. Being a part of a team of skilled individuals working together to create a unique application I think is the best part of the job. I have worked in team settings in every job I've ever had, with coding I feel as if the work really matters. I find creating web and mobile applications to be very rewarding work. I like that I can use my skill set to bring information too people in a fun and artistic way.
